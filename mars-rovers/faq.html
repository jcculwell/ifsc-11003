Common Questions

Why are rovers sent to Mars?

Rovers allow scientists to explore the Martian surface directly, study rocks, search for signs of life, and prepare for future human exploration.

How do rovers communicate with Earth?

They use orbiters around Mars as relays to send data back to Earth, since direct communication would require enormous power.

How long do missions last?

Most rovers are designed for 90 days to 1 Earth year, but many exceed their lifespan — Curiosity and Perseverance are still active today.
