Mars Rover Mission Timeline

Curiosity Rover – 2011–Present

Launched: November 26, 2011 | Landed: August 6, 2012

Major Achievements: Discovered evidence of ancient freshwater streams and organic molecules on Mars.

Perseverance Rover – 2020–Present

Launched: July 30, 2020 | Landed: February 18, 2021

Major Achievements: Collected rock cores, deployed Ingenuity helicopter, and explored Jezero Crater.
